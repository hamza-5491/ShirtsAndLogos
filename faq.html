Frequently Asked Questions

What is dye sublimation?

Dye sublimation is a heat transfer process that imprints your logos and graphics into the fibers of 100% polyester fabric to essentially make the designs part of the fabric. SHIRTSandLOGOS uses the process to create custom wearable garments and many other hard goods.

What are the advantages of dye sublimation over screen printing?

  • Logos and numbers will NOT fade, crack or peel
  • More detailed designs can be imprinted
  • Unlimited amount of colors can be used
  • Names and numbers don’t stick to your back when you sweat
  • Colors on items stay vibrant wash after wash – year after year
  • Reordering is simple – the minimum is always 1 piece

What is the difference between semi custom and full custom sublimation?

Semi custom is the process of taking an existing light colored garment and heat pressing a front and back logo. The printing area is limited to approximately 12″ wide by 16″ long.

Full custom sublimation allows you to create an “all-over” design – a completely custom garment with any colors, designs, effects and patterns you request.

What is the difference between semi custom and full custom pants?

Semi custom pants are offered in white, black, navy, or gray based color. The side panel area and belt loops can be customized with whatever sublimated “braid”, pattern, or text you request. Sponsor logos and player numbers may also be sublimated to these areas. Standard inseam length is 36″ unless specified by you.

Full custom pants are created in our warehouse and are ideal for matching any color used on your jersey. You can customize every inch of the pant with an pattern, text, logo etc. These pants can be made (in 2″ increments) from a 22″ waist all the way up to a 60″ waist and can have any inseam length. For optimal fit please order your jean size.

Artwork fees will be charged on an if-needed basis. If you have a design/artwork question, please discuss with your sales rep. We do NOT charge a setup fee for hats

There may be setup/screen fees for screen print and embroidery.

What is the minimum order?

For sublimated garments and accessories the minimum is alway one. For hats, screen print and embroidered items the minimum is twelve.

How can I check on the status of my order?

Typical orders leave our facility in 15 business days after receiving your final artwork approval and payment. During our peak season (January-June) we experience a large volume of orders and it may lengthen production time. If your order has taken longer than 15 business days, you can contact your sales rep via phone or email. They should be able to tell you the date your items were printed, pressed and cut out. When your order ships you will automatically receive an email with the tracking information.

ABOUT US

ShirtsandLogos was founded in 1993 by Rick Marino. Originally started as a graphics company ShirtsandLogos grew into a premier garment manufacturer utilizing the new process of dye sublimation. This process set a new standard for softball and other sport teams to have fully customized uniforms. All sublimated products are made in the USA at the Milwaukee, WI location
